Aloo Parantha Recipe

Description: A popular North Indian breakfast made with wheat flour stuffed with spiced mashed potatoes. Best served hot with butter, curd and pickle.

Ingredients:-
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Fresh coriander chopped
  • Butter for cooking
Steps for making Tea:-
  1. Mash boiled potatoes in a bowl
  2. Place potato filling in centre and seal edges
  3. Add salt, chilli powder, cumin and coriander to potatoes
  4. Place potato filling in centre and seal edges
  5. Knead wheat flour into soft dough using water
  6. Place potato filling in centre and seal edges
  7. Roll dough into small circles
  8. Place potato filling in centre and seal edges
  9. Roll again into flat parantha
  10. Cook on hot pan with butter until golden brown on both sides
  11. Serve hot with curd or pickle
