Coffee RECIPE

DESCRIPTION:A simple and strong black coffee. Quick to make and perfect for an early morning energy boost.

Ingredients:-
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon instant coffee
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
Steps for making Coffee:-
  1. Boil water in a pan
  2. Add coffee and sugar to a cup
  3. Pour hot water into the cup
  4. Stir well and serve hot
