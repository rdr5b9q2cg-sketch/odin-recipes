THE CHAI RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

Everyone likes freshly made chai.But there is a proper recipe for making a fabulous tea.Here are the list of indgredients and the steps to make a delicious chai.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons tea leaves
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 inch ginger crushed
  • 2 cardamom pods crushed

Steps for making Tea:-

  1. Boil water in a pan
  2. Add crushed ginger and cardamom
  3. Add tea leaves and boil for 2 minutes
  4. Add milk and sugar
  5. Boil for another 3 minutes
  6. Strain and serve hot
